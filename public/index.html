Monospace Renamed

Monospace has been renamed to MonkeySpace! We felt that the name Monospace deterred certain developers, because they thought the conference was only about Mono. We want to change that perception and align the conference name to it's mission. That mission, help promote and educate developers in cross-platform and open-source development in .NET.

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
